Reward Rate Equivalence Across Groups
============================================================

Grand mean: 618 ± 45 events/session

Per-group means (events/session):
  c3_smr    : 613 ± 43 (n=8)
  c3_beta   : 630 ± 61 (n=8)
  c4_smr    : 619 ± 43 (n=8)
  sham      : 613 ± 32 (n=16)
